# 6.1.8 Decide the story for expand-inside-macro

The expand builtin (builtins.rs:365) performs one expansion step from
within a macro body - recursive into the macro machinery. Initially reject
it inside compiled macros (rare; the shipped examples don't use it) or
route it back to the host expander. Document the limitation.
