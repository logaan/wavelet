# Lists as pointer-plus-length of packed elements, strings as raw UTF-8

Replace TAG_LIST boxes (arrays of box pointers) with the canonical (ptr, len)
pair over elements packed at their canonical size — list<u8> becomes an
actual byte buffer, 4-8x smaller and memcpy-able at boundaries. Strings drop
their box header and become raw UTF-8 (ptr, len).
